LINKING FOR DUMMIES (II)

If you want the machine to be turned ON when several
buttons are ON, write their numbers separated by "and"
as follows:

  123 and 456 and 789

If you want the machine to be turned ON when one of 
several buttons are ON and OFF when none of then are,
write their numbers separated by "or":

  123 or 456 or 789

If you want the machine to be turned OFF when several
buttons are ON, write their numbers separated by "and"
but insert "not" before everything:

  not (123 and 456 and 789)
